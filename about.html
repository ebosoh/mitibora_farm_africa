About Us

We believe in the power of community-driven environmental restoration. Our innovative approach combines poverty alleviation with reforestation, creating sustainable solutions for both people and planet.

Our Story

Founded in Kenya, Miti Bora Farm Africa emerged from a simple yet powerful idea: what if we could address two critical challenges simultaneouslyâ€”poverty and deforestation?

We partner with families in rural communities, providing them with the opportunity to earn income through tree seedling production. In return, we offer school fee loans, ensuring their children can access quality education while contributing to environmental restoration.

Our innovative approach combines poverty alleviation with reforestation, creating sustainable solutions for both people and planet. Our partnerships with international organizations, embassies, and energy companies create a sustainable funding model that benefits everyone involved.
