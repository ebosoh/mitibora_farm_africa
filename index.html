About Mitibora Farm

We partner with families in rural communities, providing them with the opportunity to earn income through tree seedling production. In return, we offer school fee loans, ensuring their children can access quality education while contributing to environmental restoration.

Tree Seedling Production

We provide families with seeds, training, and resources to produce high-quality tree seedlings.

School Fees Loans

We provide interest-free loans to families for their children's education, repaid through seedling production instead of cash payments.

Community Development

Beyond individual families, we work to strengthen entire communities through environmental restoration and economic empowerment.
